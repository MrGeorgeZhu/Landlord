Goal: Get rid of all your cards first.

1. Single
2. Double(2 of a kind)
3. Trio(3 of a kind)
4. Bomb(4 of a kind & 2 jokers)
The move you make have to follow the previous pattern and be bigger than it.
If the other two players do not have bigger cards, the current player gets to lead the next round.

*The cards are sorted from the biggest to smallest for your convenience.
*The suit of the card does not really matter in this game.
